Chicken Lasagna

This is one of the rarest recipes that you will come across

The recipe includes ingredients and a walkthrough on how to
apply the same to come up with a delicacy

Ingredients
  • Milk, 1 litre
  • 2 Eggs
  • Salt
  • Tomatoes
  • Flour
Steps:
  1. Pour the milk in a jar
  2. Add 500g of flour
  3. add two teaspoons of salt
  4. Add two eggs
  5. Mix untill the dough is ready
  6. Decorate the dough with tomatoes
  7. Place the complete dough in the oven and wait untill completely cooked
